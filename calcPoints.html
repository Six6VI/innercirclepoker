This is the algorithm that determines how many points you get from each tournament. Your top 5 tournaments will be added together for your total score for the season.

Points = SQRT((n*b*(b/e))/(f+1.0))

  • SQRT= The square root
  • n= The total number of players
  • b= The cost of the buy-in
  • e= Your total expense(buy-in,re-buys,add-ons)
  • f= Your finishing place
